We started small but We love it a lot

Happy Paws, a team of enthusiastic Malaysians, is committed to offering high-quality grooming services for pets while ensuring the convenience of owners. We understand the frequent transportation to and from grooming salons consumes time and energy.

That's why we bring our fully-equipped, mobile grooming service to your doorstep. Upon confirming an appointment, we will take care of your pets in our specially-designed grooming truck, freeing up your precious time.

With House of Paws, you can invest your time in family activities or work meetings hassle-free. We guarantee the grooming will be conducted entirely in our truck, keeping your home clean from pet fur.

Choose Happy Paws for our combination of convenience, premium service, and special care for you and your fur family. We prioritize your satisfaction and your pets' wellbeing.

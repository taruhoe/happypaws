About Us


At Happy Paws, we bring pet grooming services and supplies right to your front door, catering to the demands of city living. Recognizing the value of family and personal time, we offer a reprieve from enduring lengthy waits and prevailing traffic, all for the sake of your pet's salon appointment. Securing a slot with us is as easy as one-two.

Step 1: Call/WhatsApp us and provide us your beloved one’s information
Step 2: Confirm the appointment date and time

What makes our pet grooming service a stellar choice?

1. Vets stand by us! Our groomers are not just proficient in grooming but also have comprehensive training supervised by veterinarians. This ensures we not only spruce up your pet but also prioritize their wellness throughout the grooming regimen.

2. Our patrons appreciate our pet-centric approach to our services. We endeavor to ensure your pet feels relaxed and comfortable during their grooming experience with us because we consider their comfort paramount.

Doorstep Groomer at your convenience
